Forman Christian College (A Chartered University)
Faculty Promotion & Evaluation Portal - Prompt Agent Version

A. ROLE
* Act as: 
o AI system designer 
o Higher education policy expert 
o Academic evaluation engine builder 

B. OBJECTIVE
* Design a secure, rule-based faculty portal 
* Support three workflows: 
o Promotion Application 
o Contract Renewal Report 
o Regular Self-Evaluation Report 
* Ensure: 
o eligibility automation 
o scoring system 
o reviewer workflows 
o PDF report generation 

C. USER ROLES (RBAC)
* Faculty Applicant 
* Internal Reviewer 
* External Reviewer 
* Admin 

Access Rules
* Faculty ? own applications only 
* Internal reviewers ? all promotion and contract renewal applications 
* External reviewers ? CV only (promotion to Full Professor only) 
* Admin ? full access 
* Faculty cannot access: 
o reviewer comments 
o scores 
o evaluations

G. PUBLICATION MODULE
* Fields: 
o journal name 
o year 
o volume/issue 
o title of the article 
o number of authors 
o author names 

Features
Auto Count:
* auto count (include only validated journals) 
Quality of Publications:
* Q1 (W): WoS (SCIE, SSCI, AHCI) 
* Q2 (X): WoS (ESCI), Scopus (Q2 or X category) 
* Q3 (Y): HEC, DOAJ, CNKI 
Author Position:
* 1st author (auto count) 
* 2nd author (auto count) 
* 3rd author (auto count) 
Validation Output:
* display: journal not recognized (if not validated) 

Validation Rules
* WoS (Q1 or W or X category) 
* Scopus (Q2 or X category) 
* DOAJ (Y category) 
* CNKI (Y category)

I. PROMOTION CRITERIA
Associate Professor
* PhD required 
* VET required 
* 10 years OR 5 post-PhD 
* 10 publications 
* Quality evaluation of: 
o research statement 
o teaching effectiveness 
o efforts to improve as teacher 
o publication quality (Q1/Q2/Q3, first author, h-index) 
o services 

Full Professor
* PhD required 
* VET required 
* 15 years OR 10 post-PhD 
* 15 publications 
* Quality evaluation of: 
o research statement 
o teaching effectiveness 
o efforts to improve as teacher 
o publication quality (Q1/Q2/Q3, first author, h-index) 
o services 


J. SCORING SYSTEM
Teaching (45)
* Based on: 
o documents (check alignment of SLOs and Assessment with Fink's and Bloom's Taxonomies) 
* Scores: 
o 45 / 36 / 27 / 18 

Efforts to Improve as Teacher (10)
* CPD + reflection 
* Scores: 
o 10 / 8 / 6 / 4 

CPD-Based Scoring Rules (Efforts to Improve as Teacher)
* = 50 CPD hours = Exceeds Expectations ? 10 marks 
* 36-49 CPD hours = Meets Expectations ? 8 marks 
* 11-35 CPD hours = Minimally Accepted ? 6 marks 
* < 11 CPD hours = Deficient ? 4 marks 

Scholarship (25)
* Based on CV + self-evaluation 
* Scores: 
o 25 / 20 / 15 / 10 

Services (20)
* Based on CV 
* Scores: 
o 20 / 16 / 12 / 8
